How GOVVENS Works

A simple, secure, and seamless journey from signup to stadium entry

1
Create Account

Sign up with your email and phone number. Quick registration takes less than 2 minutes. Your account is your gateway to secure ticketing.

2
Identity Verification

Complete one-time eKYC verification using Aadhaar. Then perform face/eye scan to link your biometrics to your account. This ensures ticket security.

3
Browse Events

Explore upcoming cricket matches at Chinnaswamy Stadium. View match details, dates, times, and ticket prices. Subscribe to get early notifications.

4
View Match Details

Check detailed match information including weather forecast, stadium map, gate occupancy levels, and crowd expectations. Plan your visit accordingly.

5
Select Your Seats

Choose your preferred seats from the interactive stadium map. Select multiple seats for friends and family. Seats are held for 15 minutes during checkout.

6
Secure Payment

Pay securely via Razorpay using Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, or Wallets. All transactions are encrypted and PCI-DSS compliant.

7
Receive Ticket

Get instant confirmation with a unique QR code ticket. Access your tickets anytime from "My Tickets" section. QR code is face-locked to you.

8
Get Entry Schedule

Receive your personalized entry time slot and assigned gate via SMS and email. AI optimizes your slot to minimize waiting time and crowd density.

9
Plan Your Journey

Enter your home PIN code to get optimized route and departure time. Google Maps integration calculates the best travel time to arrive during your slot.

10
Venue Entry

Arrive at your assigned gate during your time slot. Quick face/eye scan verification (similar to DigiYatra) confirms your identity and grants entry.

11
Enjoy the Match

Watch live gate occupancy and crowd heatmap in real-time. Get exit time notifications to avoid post-match congestion. Safe, organized, and enjoyable experience.

Platform Features

Early Notification System

Pay a small refundable fee (₹10) to get SMS alerts when tickets open. This ensures fair and early access. The fee is deductible from your ticket price.

Secure Verification

Aadhaar-based eKYC and facial recognition ensure each ticket is tied to the user. Backup verification uses mobile OTP and ID at entry gates.

Staggered Entry/Exit

Automated time slot assignment minimizes congestion and stampede risk. Each user gets personalized entry and exit windows via SMS and app notifications.

Privacy-First Location

Only your home PIN code is required. Routes and timings are computed using Google Maps API. We never track your live location.

Group Bookings

Book seats for friends and family. The seat map supports multiple selections, holding seats for up to 15 minutes during checkout.

Secure Payments

Payments processed securely via Razorpay. Accepts Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and Wallets. All transactions are encrypted.

Common Questions

What is Govvens?

Govvens is a civic-tech ticketing and crowd-safety platform that manages crowd entry, ticketing, and exit for large events using staggered timings and verified ticketing.

Learn More
How does verification work?

Each ticket is tied to the user through Aadhaar-based eKYC and facial recognition. At the venue, you'll go through a quick face/eye scan similar to DigiYatra.

Do I need to share my live location?

No. Only your home PIN code is required. Routes and timings are computed using Google Maps API based on that. Your privacy is protected.

What if payment fails?

The reserved seats are released after 15 minutes if payment isn't completed. You can retry the payment within the 15-minute window.
