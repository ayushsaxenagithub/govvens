Verification Guidelines

Proper Lighting

Ensure good, even lighting. Avoid harsh shadows on your face. Natural daylight or white LED lighting works best.

Camera Position

Keep your face centered in the frame. Position camera at eye level, about 12-18 inches away.

Natural Expression

Look directly at the camera with a neutral or slightly smiling expression. Blink naturally.

Accessories

Remove large sunglasses and hats if possible. Avoid face masks or heavy makeup changes from your ticket photo.

Liveness Check

You may be asked to blink, nod, or smile. This ensures you're a real person (not a photo).

How Long It Takes

Typical verification: 5-10 seconds. First-time users may take up to 30 seconds for biometric enrollment.
